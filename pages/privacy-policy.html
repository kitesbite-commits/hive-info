Privacy Policy

📅 Effective: June 2, 2026  |  📍 Ather Hive

Ather Hive values your privacy. This policy describes what information we collect, how we protect it, and your rights. By using Ather Hive, you agree to the practices described here.

📋 1. Information We Collect

To provide a social chat experience, we collect minimal data required for functionality and security.

🔐 Account info Username, hashed password, email address (only for recovery).
🖼️ Profile Optional avatar image & short bio.
💬 Messages Public room chats & private messages (encrypted in transit).
🤝 Social Friends, likes, and feed interactions.
📱 Device & IP Device ID & IP address at registration (to prevent abuse).

We do not collect precise location, contacts, or browsing history. All sensitive data (passwords, tokens) are cryptographically protected.

🔒 2. How We Store & Protect Data

Your data is stored on secure servers (Google Cloud) and protected using industry‑standard practices:

  • ✅ Passwords are never stored in plain text – we use strong one-way hashing (bcrypt).
  • ✅ All network traffic (chat, avatars) is encrypted with TLS/WSS – no eavesdropping.
  • ✅ API authentication uses signed JSON Web Tokens (JWT).
  • ✅ Regular security updates and limited access to servers.

While no system is 100% invulnerable, we take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorised access.

🗑️ 3. Data Retention & Deletion

We keep your data only as long as necessary to provide the service:

  • Room chats Last 100 messages per public room are stored. Older ones are automatically removed.
  • Private messages Last 30 messages per conversation are kept; old ones disappear automatically.
  • Account You can request deletion. After 30 days, your account and all associated data (messages, friends, avatar) are permanently erased.

To delete your account, send an email to support@ather-hive.duckdns.org with your username and registered email. You’ll receive confirmation within 7 days.

👥 4. Sharing Information with Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Exceptions only if:

  • ⚖️ Required by law (court order, legal obligation).
  • 🛡️ To protect our rights, prevent fraud, or ensure safety of users.

We never share your messages or private data with advertisers or analytics firms.

🧒 5. Children’s Privacy

Ather Hive is intended for users 13 years or older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has registered, contact us immediately and we will remove the account.

🌍 6. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • 📥 Access – request a copy of your data.
  • ✏️ Correct – update username, bio, avatar in the app.
  • 🗑️ Delete – request permanent account deletion (30‑day process).
  • 🚫 Object – stop certain processing (e.g., feed personalisation).

To exercise your rights, email support@ather-hive.duckdns.org. We will respond within 30 days.

🍪 7. Cookies & Local Storage

Our mobile app does not use tracking cookies. We may store a secure authentication token locally on your device – this token is used only to keep you logged in. You can clear app data at any time via your device settings.

📢 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

If we make significant changes, we will notify you through:

  • 📱 In‑app notice (when you open the app).
  • 📧 Email (if you provided one).

The latest version will always be available at this URL. Continued use of Ather Hive after changes means you accept the updated policy.

📬 9. Contact Us

For privacy questions, data requests, or concerns:

📧 support@ather-hive.duckdns.org

We aim to reply within 5‑7 business days.
