Privacy Policy

Effective: June 2, 2026 | Last revised: June 2026 | Ather Hive

Ather Hive is committed to transparency and data minimalism. This policy explains how we collect, store, and protect your information when you use our platform. By using Ather Hive, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information we collect

We only collect the data necessary to deliver a functional, secure, and social experience. No unnecessary tracking or surveillance.

Account identity Username, hashed password, recovery email (optional but used for deletion).
Profile data Avatar image (optional) and short biography.
Messages & content Public room chats, private messages (encrypted in transit).
Social graph Friends list, likes, feed interactions.
Security metadata IP address at registration, device fingerprint (to prevent abuse).

We do not collect precise location, contacts, payment information, or browsing history outside the app. All credentials are hashed or encrypted.

2. How we store & protect your data

Your privacy is protected using modern security infrastructure:

  • encryption TLS 1.3 for all network traffic (WebSockets & API) – no third-party eavesdropping.
  • password hashing bcrypt (cost factor 12) – never stored in plaintext.
  • access control Strict internal policies, signed JWTs, and limited database access.
  • infrastructure Secure cloud environment (Google Cloud) with regular updates and monitoring.

Although no system is completely immune, we adopt industry-leading countermeasures to mitigate data breaches.

3. Data retention & deletion

We retain information only as long as needed to provide the service or comply with legal obligations.

  • public rooms Last 100 messages per room are stored; older ones rotate automatically.
  • private messages Last 30 messages per conversation are kept; excess messages are purged.
  • account data Upon verified deletion request, all associated data (username, messages, friends, avatar, feed) is permanently erased within 30 days.

To request full account deletion, send an email to kitesbite@gmail.com with your registered username and the email address linked to your account. You will receive an acknowledgement within 72 hours and final confirmation after successful removal. If you log in within 7 days after the request, the deletion is automatically canceled.

4. Sharing information with third parties

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal data. Exceptions are strictly limited to:

  • Comply with applicable laws, court orders, or legal processes.
  • Protect the security or integrity of Ather Hive (e.g., investigating abuse).
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and prevent harm to users.

Ather Hive does not integrate with third-party ad networks or analytics trackers. Your messages and identity remain confidential.

5. Children’s privacy

Ather Hive is intended for users aged 13 or older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we discover such data, we will delete the account immediately. Please contact us if you believe a child has registered.

6. Your rights & choices

Depending on your region (including GDPR, CCPA, etc.), you may request:

  • Access: Receive a copy of your personal data.
  • Rectification: Update your username, bio, or avatar via the app.
  • Erasure: Permanent account deletion (process described above).
  • Restriction / Object: Limit processing of your data (e.g., feed personalization).

To exercise your rights, email kitesbite@gmail.com with “Privacy Request” in the subject line. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Cookies & local storage

Ather Hive does not use tracking cookies or persistent identifiers for advertising. We store a secure authentication token (JWT) locally on your device to maintain your session. You can clear app data anytime from device settings, which will log you out.

8. Changes to this privacy policy

If we make significant changes to our privacy practices, we will notify you via in-app notice (visible when you open the app) and by email (if you provided one). The latest version of this policy will always be available at this page. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

For privacy questions, data requests, deletion inquiries, or concerns, please reach out to our data protection contact:

✉️ Official support & privacy channel: kitesbite@gmail.com

We aim to respond to all privacy-related inquiries within 5–7 business days. For urgent deletion matters, include “Account Deletion Request – Ather Hive” in the subject.
